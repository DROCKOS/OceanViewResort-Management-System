Logging In & Out
How to access and exit the system
▼
  • 1
    Navigate to http://localhost:8080 in your browser to reach the landing page.
  • 2
    Click the Staff Portal button in the top right corner of the landing page.
  • 3
    Enter your credentials. Default: Username admin · Password Admin@1234
  • 4
    Press Enter or click Sign In to Portal. You will be redirected to the Dashboard.
  • 5
    To log out, click Sign Out at the bottom of the left sidebar at any time.

Creating a Reservation
Step-by-step guide to book a guest
▼
  • 1
    Click New Reservation in the sidebar or the ＋ New Reservation button in the top bar.
  • 2
    Step 1 — Guest Details: Enter the guest's full name, address, contact number (10–15 digits), and optional email.
  • 3
    Step 2 — Select Dates: Choose check-in and check-out dates. Both must be today or in the future. Check-out must be after check-in.
  • 4
    Step 3 — Choose Room: Available rooms for the selected dates are shown as cards. Click a room card to select it.
  • 5
    Step 4 — Confirm: Review the booking summary and estimated bill total. Click Confirm Reservation to finalise.
  • 6
    A success screen will show the new Reservation ID. You can then generate the bill or add another reservation.
💡
Tip: If no rooms appear in Step 3, the selected dates are fully booked. Try different dates or check the Rooms page to verify availability.

Generating & Printing Bills
How to create and print invoices
▼
  • 1
    Go to Reservations and find the booking you want to bill. Click the Bill button in the Actions column.
  • 2
    If no bill exists yet, click Generate Bill. The system automatically calculates: Room Rate × Number of Nights.
  • 3
    Once generated, the professional invoice will appear showing guest details, room info, dates, and total amount.
  • 4
    Click Mark as Paid once the guest has paid. The bill will show a green PAID stamp.
  • 5
    Click Print Bill to open the browser print dialog. The sidebar and controls are automatically hidden for a clean printout.
💡
Tip: You can also find all bills in the Billing section in the sidebar, where you can filter by paid/pending status.

Managing Rooms
Add, edit and toggle room availability
▼
  • 1
    Go to Rooms in the sidebar to view all room cards with their current availability status.
  • 2
    Click ＋ Add Room to create a new room. Enter room number, type (Standard/Deluxe/Suite), price per night, and description.
  • 3
    Click the ✏️ Edit button on any room card to update its type, price, description, or availability status.
  • 4
    Use the Set Unavailable / Set Available toggle button on each card for quick availability switching without editing.
💡
Note: A room marked as Unavailable will not appear in the room selection step when creating new reservations, even if no booking exists for those dates.
❌
Cancelling a Reservation
How to cancel an existing booking
▼
  • 1
    Go to Reservations and locate the booking to cancel.
  • 2
    Click the Cancel button in the Actions column, or open the reservation and click Cancel Reservation.
  • 3
    A confirmation modal will appear. Click Yes, Cancel It to confirm. This action cannot be undone.
  • 4
    The reservation status will change to CANCELLED and the room will become available for new bookings.
⚠️
Warning: Cancellation is permanent. If a bill has already been generated for the reservation, it will remain in the system but the reservation will be marked cancelled.

Reports & Analytics
Understanding the reports page
▼
  • 1
    The Reports page shows live analytics calculated directly from all reservation data.
  • 2
    The Status Doughnut shows the ratio of confirmed vs cancelled reservations at a glance.
  • 3
    The Room Type Bar Chart shows which room types (Standard, Deluxe, Suite) are most frequently booked.
  • 4
    The Reservation Trend Line shows how bookings have grown or changed over time by month.
  • 5
    The Top Guests table shows the most frequent visitors ranked by number of bookings and total nights stayed.
🔍
Audit Logs
Tracking system activity
▼
  • 1
    Every action in the system (login, reservation creation, billing, cancellation) is automatically recorded with a timestamp.
  • 2
    The Audit Logs page shows the last 100 actions. Use the search bar to find specific actions or users.
  • 3
    Use the Action filter dropdown to view only a specific type of action, such as all logins or all cancellations.
  • 4
    Click the ↻ Refresh button to reload the latest logs at any time.
